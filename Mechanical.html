Paper Presentation

Rules

Co-ordinators


Team size is restricted to maximum of two members.
The paper must be typed single-spaced in 12-point Times New Roman or Arial font and be no more than 12 pages. Only text directly related to the item being presented and any accompanying illustrations, tables, figures, etc., will count toward the 12-page limit. Title pages, tables of contents and bibliographies shall not be counted toward the limit.
All papers should have a bibliography/works-cited page and appropriate footnotes (any format, such as MLA, APA, etc., as long as its use is consistent throughout the document, will be acceptable). Papers omitting a bibliography or using copied material will be automatically disqualified.
Photographs should be treated as being equivalent to figures, with the caption being placed at the bottom of the photograph. Photocopies of photographs are not acceptable.
The contestants are required to send only a soft copy of their entry along with an abstract (not more than 100 words) to the college web site: www.fetr.ac.in with subject “paper presentation” Please title your entry in the following format: (teamname)_(institute)
Presentation
Each team/entrant must formally present his or her paper at the day of the event. Presentations must be no longer than 10 minutes.
A 5-minute question-and-answer period will follow the presentation of the paper. Questions may be posed by judges and/or students attending the presentation.
Presentations may be accompanied by visual aids.
Registration
Registration Fees: 50/- Per student.
Judging Criteria
Judging will be on a basis of 100, as follows
A. Technical Paper (50):
Choice of Topic (5), Technical Content (30), Grammar and Spelling (5), Paper Appearance and format (10)
B. Presentation (50):
Content (20), Quality of Presentation (20), Question and Answer (10)
